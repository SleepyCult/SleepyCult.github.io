Alter types:

ANP. apparently normal parts
EP, emotional parts
MP, Mixed parts

Core:Original personality/being

Host/CoHost: Alter that fronts the most or deals with most of life. normally anp

Protector: Protects from more trauma, made from trauma, or caries trauma, normally ep

Littles, Middles, Bigs: Littles, child alters, Middles, preteens or teens, Bigs, adults, all can be anp or ep

Caretaker: Takes care of system, ether emotional care for alters, physical care for the body, or general environmental care. Normally developed because of no one outside of the system is caring for them or because the system cannot care for itself, Normally EP

Sub type of Caretaker: Internal Self helpers: Rarely come out but care for headspace and alters.

Gatekeeps: Controls who fronts and memories. normally EPS

Interjects: Any external person, character or object that may be brought into a system. normally abuser based or fictionally based, Can be ether ep or anp

Persecutors: Normally seen as the bad or evil alters. Turns abuse back in on the system and hurts themself and other alters purposefully, intentions are for the system to grown stronger from the self-inflicted harm to be stronger than any abusers, sometimes even attacking them for specific traits like being transgender or homosexual, thinking it will help remove that trait from the system to make it no longer a possible way to be abused, often intentions are for the best and want to make it so that the system can grow pas whatever is causing issues.

Sexual alters: ep, normally to front well for sexual abuse and interactions

Nonhuman: developed mainly when told, treated and programed to think that they are a specific object, animal, or nonhuman being.
